Privacy

Your data stays on your device.

Swipe In does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. Everything lives on your phone — always.

  • No analytics or tracking SDKs
  • No advertising networks
  • No account required — ever
  • Stored using Apple's Core Data framework
  • Works fully offline
  • Data only leaves your device if you export it yourself

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

What data does Swipe In collect?

Swipe In does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All information you enter — time entries, activities, places, and preferences — is stored exclusively on your device using Apple's Core Data framework and UserDefaults.

Does the app share data with third parties?

No. Swipe In does not include any advertising SDKs, analytics frameworks, or third-party tracking libraries. No data ever leaves your device unless you explicitly export it yourself using the CSV export feature.

What data is stored locally?

  • Your time entries (start time, end time, activity label, place label)
  • Your custom activity and place lists
  • App preferences (accent color, appearance mode, selected icon)
  • Tutorial completion state

CSV Export

When you use the export feature, a CSV file is generated on your device and shared via the iOS share sheet. It is your responsibility to decide where you send or save that file. The app has no involvement after you make that choice.

Children's privacy

Swipe In does not collect any data from any user, including children under 13.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes in a future update, the updated version will be posted here with a new date.

Common questions

Is Swipe In really free?

Yes — no ads, no premium tier. If it saves you time, consider supporting development via Ko-Fi.

Does it work offline?

Fully. Everything is stored locally on your device. No internet connection needed at any point.

How do I export my data?

Go to History → tap the export icon. A CSV file is generated on-device and shared via the iOS share sheet.

Can I edit a past entry?

Yes — tap any entry in your history to edit the start time, end time, activity, or location.

Will there be an Android version?

Not currently planned — iOS is the focus for now as a solo developer.

Where is my data backed up?

Your data is included in your iPhone's standard iCloud backup automatically. Dedicated iCloud sync is coming soon.
